About Us

Your Pajama can speak! For parents who have time to tell bedtime stories or not, as well as who traveled a lot and barely have time for bedtime stories, Paja-magic is an exciting tool to interact with your children and always be a good companion. As a starting up business, Paja-magic is trying to provide unique life experiences to those 25 to 45-year-old parents who have children around 3-8. We also welcome those story lovers to have contributions to our magic pajamas. Those designed and cute stickers also enables parents and children to create special and fairytale pajamas everyday.
